About MacBook

The MacBook is a line of Macintosh laptop computers designed by Apple.
The MacBook line consists of the original MacBook (2006-present), the MacBook Pro (2006-present), and the MacBook Air (2008-present).
The MacBook lineup came from the merging of the PowerBook and iBook lines as Apple
transitioned over to using Intel instead of PowerPC processors. Click a link below for more information on each type of MacBook.
